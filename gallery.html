The Flying Thing

I'm working on building a radio-controlled tricopter. I like flying things in general, but being a software guy I am fascinated by the programming challenges involved in stabilizing a fly-by-wire machine. So I thought I'd build a test bed that I can get my feet wet with. Right now it's using a third-party Flight Control Board, but once I get it flying I plan to yank that out and plug in an Arduino with home-rolled software. Hopefully I won't crash the thing through some neighbor's roof in the process.

To control the animation, hold the SHIFT key and press:

  • Up Arrow to increase aspect ratio
  • Down Arrow to decrease aspect ratio
  • Right and Left Arrows to switch direction
Use the mouse to control the movement. Hit ESC to stop rotation.
